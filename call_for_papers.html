Call for Papers

EurIPS 2025 Workshop: Foundation Models for Multimodal Health Signals

Overview

We invite submissions on the development, application, and evaluation of foundation models and large language models for multimodal health signals. We welcome both methodological contributions and applications in healthcare and biomedical domains.

Topics of Interest

Topics include but are not limited to:

  • Novel architectures for multimodal foundation models in healthcare
  • Techniques for aligning heterogeneous health data modalities
  • Self-supervised learning approaches for health signals
  • Transfer learning and domain adaptation for health applications
  • Multimodal representation learning for clinical decision support
  • Integration of knowledge graphs with multimodal health models
  • Temporal modeling of multimodal health data
  • Interpretability and explainability methods for multimodal health models
  • Fairness, bias, and equity considerations in multimodal health AI
  • Privacy-preserving techniques for multimodal health data
  • Clinical validation and evaluation of multimodal foundation models
  • Real-world applications and case studies

Submission Guidelines

We will accept two types of contributions:

  • Full papers (6-8 pages): Original research, position papers, or case studies
  • Extended abstracts (4 pages): Work in progress, clinical perspectives, or demonstration proposals

All submissions must be in PDF format and follow the EurIPS 2025 format. Submissions will be reviewed in a double-blind process, so please ensure that your paper does not contain any information that identifies the authors or their institutions.

Papers should be submitted through OpenReview. We will implement double-blind review with 3 reviewers per paper. The Programme Committee consists of 20 confirmed reviewers with expertise spanning ML, clinical medicine, and health informatics.

Accepted papers will be presented at the workshop either as oral presentations or posters, and will be published in the workshop proceedings.

Important Dates

Submission Deadline: 10 October 2025 (Anywhere on Earth)

Review Period: 11 October - 25 October 2025

Notification: 26 October 2025

Camera-Ready Deadline: 11 November 2025

Workshop Date: December 6 or 7, 2025

All deadlines are at 11:59 PM Anywhere on Earth (AoE).
